Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I brush my teeth?

Brush at least twice a day, morning and night, for two minutes each time.

What toothpaste is best for sensitive teeth?

Look for toothpaste specially formulated for sensitive teeth, like Colgate Sensitive.

Does mouthwash replace brushing?

No, mouthwash complements brushing but does not replace it. Brushing removes plaque physically.
